## Rough Itinerary

### China — ~23 days (Sep 26 – Oct 19)
**Fly:** SFO → Beijing (carry-on only feasible, Oct weather is mild 15-25°C)

**Golden Week warning:** Oct 1-7 is national holiday. Zhangjiajie is the Golden Week hideout — large enough park to absorb crowds, far better than being in Beijing.

**Transport:** Mostly high-speed rail. Two short domestic flights recommended: Beijing→Zhangjiajie (~2hr) and Chengdu→Guangzhou (~2hr). Everything else by train.

| Segment | Dates | Nights | Anchor Experience |
|---|---|---|---|
| Beijing | Sep 26 - Oct 1 | 5 | Great Wall, Forbidden City, hutongs, Summer Palace |
| Zhangjiajie | Oct 1 - 5 | 4 | Avatar mountains, Bailong elevator, hiking (Golden Week hideout) |
| Chongqing | Oct 5 - 8 | 3 | Cyberpunk vertical city, authentic Chongqing hot pot |
| Chengdu | Oct 8 - 11 | 3 | Pandas, Sichuan teahouses, dan dan noodles |
| Guangzhou | Oct 11 - 13 | 2 | Cantonese dim sum, authentic local food culture |
| Shanghai | Oct 13 - 19 | 3 | The Bund, French Concession, futuristic skyline, exit city |

**Fly:** Shanghai → Osaka Oct 19
