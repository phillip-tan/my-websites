🇰🇷 Seoul — Gangnam Aesthetic Work Sep 26 – Oct 7 · 11 days
Korea — specifically the Gangnam district of Seoul — is the global leader in aesthetic medicine. Clinics are staffed by board-certified plastic surgeons and dermatologists who perform thousands of procedures per year. Prices run 30–50% below US rates with no compromise on technique or technology. Many clinics have English-speaking coordinators and handle foreign patients daily. The plan: front-load procedures on days 1–6, let day 7–11 be recovery + sightseeing. Leave for China Oct 8 with fresh skin.
☀️ Sun Spots — Yes, Laser Can Remove These Priority treatment
Solar lentigines (sun spots / "birthmark-like" flat brown patches) are directly targeted by pico laser — specifically PicoWay and PicoSure systems. The ultra-short pulse shatters melanin pigment without damaging surrounding tissue. In Korea, pico laser for pigmentation is one of the most-performed procedures. Expect 1–3 sessions for significant clearance. Each session is 15–30 minutes, 2–3 days of mild redness/flaking, then the spots gradually fade over 2–4 weeks. Get your first session on day 2 or 3 so the healing window falls within your Seoul stay.

⏱ Timing Notes — When to Repeat
Masseter botox: Results peak at 4–6 weeks and last 4–6 months. First treatment in Seoul will be showing results by the time you reach Osaka. Plan a top-up when back in the US ~Feb 2027.

Facial botox: Same timeline — 3–4 months. One treatment in Korea covers the whole trip.

Pico laser: Multiple sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart give best pigmentation results. You can do session 1 in Seoul (Sep 28), session 2 optionally in Thailand (Dec — clinics in Chiang Mai also do pico), and the spots will continue fading through January.

RF microneedling / Rejuran: Results build over 4–8 weeks post-treatment. You'll be seeing the full benefit by the time you're in Japan.

Note on sun exposure: After laser treatments, strict SPF 50 is critical for 4–6 weeks. Wear a hat in Vietnam and Cambodia especially — or time pico laser so it's at least 2 weeks before intense sun exposure.
